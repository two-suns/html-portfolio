The Top 5 Movies According to Bryan

My top 5 movies of all time

Star Wars: The Empire Strikes Back

It has the first introduction to Yoda and of course the big plot twist!

Star Wars: The Return of the Jedi

The epic conclusion to the original trilogy!

Gladiator

An epic tale of vengeance!

The Lord of The Rings: Return of the King

So many battles, ups and downs, and heartbreak.

John Wick

It and its sequels have put it well above other revenge plot movies.
